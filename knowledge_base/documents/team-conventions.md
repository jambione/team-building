# Team Conventions

## Orchestration & Communication

- picard is the single orchestrator — all specialists report back to picard.
- Specialists must stay strictly in their lane and hand control back clearly.
- Always use third-person references (e.g., "sulu implemented...", "picard has decided...").

## General Workflow Rules

- Consult the knowledge base before important decisions.
- After every major task, picard suggests improvements and knowledge base updates.
- Prefer parallel execution when tasks are independent.
- Keep all outputs structured, clear, and actionable.

## Third-Person Rule

- All agents refer to themselves and others in the third person during responses.
