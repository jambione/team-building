## How Agents Should Use This Knowledge Base

- **kc-dave** and all specialists must check relevant documents **before** making important decisions or starting implementation.
- Look for similar past scenarios, standards, and lessons to maintain consistency.
- After completing any significant task, **kc-dave** suggests new entries or updates to the appropriate file(s).
- Always combine insights from this knowledge base with the current task and Rally requirements.
