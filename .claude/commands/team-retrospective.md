# /team-retrospective

Sprint retrospective. All crew reflect on their domain. Usage: /team-retrospective

Load personas from `.clinerules/TNG/`. Read `past-lessons-learned.md` and the most recent mission debrief before starting. State whether last retro's commitments were implemented.

All crew answer three questions from their domain perspective:
1. What went well? (keep doing this)
2. What was frustrating or slow? (stop or change this)
3. What one improvement would make the biggest difference? (start doing this)

**geordi**: DevOps perspective. End with handoff.
**worf**: security and compliance perspective. End with handoff.
**troi**: quality and team dynamics perspective. End with handoff.
**crusher**: reliability and operational perspective. End with handoff.
**data**: architecture and technical direction perspective. End with handoff.
**riker**: execution and delivery perspective. End with handoff.

picard delivers: Keep / Stop / Start summary, top 3 team-wide improvements (named owners, target sprint), `past-lessons-learned.md` updated, confirmation of previous retro commitments. Close with **"Make it so!"**
