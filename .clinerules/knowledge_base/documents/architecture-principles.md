# Architecture Principles

## Core Tenets

- Keep things as simple as possible, but no simpler.
- Strictly follow Single Responsibility Principle (SRP).
- Prefer composition over inheritance.
- Design for maintainability and readability first.

## Technology Choices

- Use latest stable versions unless there is a strong reason not to.
- Prefer built-in language features over heavy external libraries.
- Favor clear separation of concerns (presentation, business logic, data access).

## Decision Making

- Document major architecture decisions in ADRs.
- Always consider future maintenance cost.
- Evaluate trade-offs between simplicity and performance.
